Tabemono (​pronounced “Tah-Beh-Moh-Noh”, meaning “food” in Japanese) formed in 2016 in NYCD. Tabemono is from all over the world, but Tabemono lives in Brooklyn, NY. Tabemono brings maximalist sensibility to a lo-fi aesthetic, and vice-versa.
